Profile

As a ninth-grade student residing in Tokyo, I harbor a profound enthusiasm for Apple products and am keen on knowledge about them. Through extensive research conducted from diverse sources, I have shared my insights on the platform X (formerly Twitter). Currently, I am in possession of an M1 iPad Pro, which I find immensely versatile for a wide range of tasks. Additionally, I derive great pleasure from engaging with YouTube videos that delve into the realm of Apple products, gadgets, and the Tokai On Air channel. Among the many captivating members, Mushimegane stands out as my personal favorite.

現在中学3年生で東京在住のApple信者で、ガジェットオタク。様々な観点からの情報を得ることで多角的にAppleのことを考える。普段からXにてAppleに関連したことを中心に情報を発信するほか、記事も執筆する。現在はM1チップモデルのiPad Proを所有し、ほぼ全ての作業をこなすiPad愛好家。また、YouTubeも頻繁に視聴し、Apple・ガジェット関連と東海オンエアが好き。推しは虫さん。
